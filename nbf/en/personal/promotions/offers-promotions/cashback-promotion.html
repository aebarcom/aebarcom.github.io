Earn up to AED 300 cashback at the summer sales

Valid Until 30 September 2016

This summer, the more you shop, the more you earn. Make even bigger savings at the summer sales with your NBF credit card and earn cashback as you shop.

Classic Credit Card Promotion

Shop up a storm and earn AED 200 cashback for AED 3,000 or more you spend on your NBF Classic Credit Card every month.

Platinum Credit Card Promotion

Now there’s no reason to hold back at the summer sales. We will give you AED 300 bonus cashback for AED 10,000 or more you spend on your NBF Platinum Exclusive Credit Card.

The extra cashback is in addition to up to 2% cashback you already earn for every dirham spent on your Platinum Exclusive Credit Card.

Now there’s no reason not to pick up some great bargains at the summer sales. Credit cards at the ready!
